EEACR- Electrical Engineering Alumni and Corporate Relations          

Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Bombay, Powai

 

The EE department has launched a departmental Alumni and Corporate Relations Outreach (ACR) Initiative with support of Institute ACR office to deepen departmental engagement with alumni and industries.

We highlight and broadcast research work aligned with the Department's core values to invite alumni and industry interest in the form of projects, consortia, invited talks, entrepreneurial collaboration etc.

Department’s Core Values

·        To develop, trust and uphold human values and ethics

·        To serve humanity through education, science and technology

·        To develop the individual and the community
